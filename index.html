About Us

Hat3rick (Hattrick) is a Website, designed for Parents, who want to register their kids in a Football Camp. Hat3rick is a Summer Football Camp for Kids between the age of 7 and 12; who are passionate about Football and are keen on improving their Skills, learning the Art of the Game while having Fun. Our well trained Coaches, with approved Training License, will help your child with specific football oriented training exercise, to master the essential skills of fooball. These Coaches are also keen on teaching the children the basic football technique as well as the general understanding of the Game. Some of our Goals are to help the Children, develop Mental Toughness, learn the art of Discipline, Consistency, be Respectful towards everyone, learn the Skills of Team-work.
